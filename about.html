About Home Plumbing USA

Connecting home and business owners nationwide with vetted, independent local plumbing experts in real-time.

Establishing Nationwide Plumbing Trust Standards

Finding a trustworthy plumber during an emergency (like a midnight burst sewer line or slab leak) is difficult. Many national registries lists are polluted with unverified entries or lead brokers. Home Plumbing USA was founded in 2024 to establish a vetted, audited matching hotline that connects property owners with certified, local plumbing coordinators who manage verified crews.

We do not operate trucks ourselves. We operate the central routing technology and database that filters independent local companies. By background checking technicians, verifying active state licensing, and reviewing liability coverage, we insulate home and business owners from unqualified handymen.
