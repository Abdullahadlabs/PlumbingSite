How often should I have my water heater serviced in Miami?

We recommend professional water heater service at least once a year in Miami (33155). Servicing includes flushing the tank to remove sediment buildup, testing the relief valve, and checking the anode rod to prevent tank rust.

What tools do technicians use to find hidden leaks in Miami?

How can I prevent water damage from plumbing failures?

Installing smart water leak detectors near water heaters, washing machines, and under sinks is highly recommended. These systems alert you on your phone and can even trigger automatic shutoff valves if a leak is detected in your Miami home.
